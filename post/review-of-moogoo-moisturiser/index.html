Moogoo Skin Milk Udder Cream Review is a fab day moisturiser

Gentle on the skin and perfect as a primer for dry skin

 Posted on May 9, 2020  |  1 minutes  |  203 words  |  Lucy Rebekah  |  

Moogoo is an Australian brand of moisturiser with natural ingredients. The skin milk udder cream moisturiser is Moogoo’s original, headliner product, but they have expanded their line to include other variations e.g. full cream version for drier skin, MSM version for rosacea etc.

This moisturiser has a light texture and absorbs relatively quickly for a moisturiser. It has a slightly tacky finish soon after application and then will eventually dry down to a natural, non-shiny finish. There is no SPF.

As it is a natural formula, it has an expiry date of one year. This is accurate, I found mine got that playdough-like rancid oil smell after 16 months or so. If you are using it as your daily face and neck moisturiser, and not leaving it unopened for months like me, you should use it up before this happens. But I point this out because expiry is not something you have to worry about that much for other moisturisers.

This sits well under makeup and is a great moisturiser for normal to dry skin. It helps prevent a cakey look from your foundation. I would totally repurchase and recommend. ðŸ’š
