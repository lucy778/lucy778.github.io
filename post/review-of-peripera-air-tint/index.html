The problem with Lip Tints: Review of Peripera Airy Ink Velvet Lip Tint

 Posted on June 2, 2019  |  2 minutes  |  321 words  |  Lucy Rebekah  |  

What is Peripera Ink Airy Velvet Lip Tint? It’s basically a moussey lipstick which stains your lips. You can wear it like a liquid lipstick or remove it and just leave the tint.

How does it look? See the pics :)

Shade: #3 Sold Out Red. This is a cool red with a matte appearance. It doesn’t emphasise lip lines, it is super smoothing.

How does it wear? The Tint is long lasting but the liquid lipstick is not and has a tendency to smear and transfer. It’s also hard to apply cleanly unless you are just applying it in the centre of your lips, ombre style. I found it wore off unevenly, remaining darker at the centre of my lips. However it did not wear off in chunks like many liquid lipsticks. If you like the gradient look then you may not mind how it wears off. Even though the tint was a red or dark pink the final tint was a Barbie cool mid toned pink. I have included pics of the lip tint just after application and some hours later after going for a swim.

How does it smell? Scent: none noticeable although I have a cold currently

How does it feel? It has a really unique slippery feel to it; not sticky or moisturising. Kind of like plastic coating your lips.

How does it apply? The packaging is shaped like a tiny juice bottle with a doefoot applicator. Very cute.

Conclusion It’s a cute lip tint but I don’t like the pink colour that it fades to. This is a problem I have with every lip tint I have tried. No matter what, even if they start out orange, they end up fading to the same pink. I guess they use the same pink dye in all of them :‘(
