Review of Clio Sharp So Simple Waterproof Pencil Liner and Dose of Colors Pencil Eyeliners

Easy to use but not the best value

 Posted on May 9, 2020  |  3 minutes  |  594 words  |  Lucy Rebekah  |  

These are both super skinny retractable pencil liners. The Clio one is a bit skinnier than the Dose of Colors one but they are both tiny. So let’s start with the use case of these things. You’re on the way to work or some important date or social event, and you didn’t have time to apply liner at home before you left. Because sleep is important, dammit.

For this use case, this type of liner is perfect. You don’t have to worry about sharpening it. You don’t have to worry about going in too heavy, making a wobbly mistake, and in the race to correct it, ending up with a liner so thick it covers your entire lid. The initial line you make is so skinny, even if you double or triple the thickness in fixing mistakes, it will still be ok.

So, they are easy to use tools with a great shape. You can tightline and line waterline easily. They are non irritating, at least on my eyes. I also didn’t have any issues with the Dose of Colors pencil part snapping, but the #6 choco brown Clio pencil snapped for me when I was swatching it for this review.

Let’s talk about the colours. The Clio pencil liner colours are very soft but not necessarily natural. The choco brown color has shimmer in it, and the cacao brown colour is a warm, medium brown. The Dose of Colors liner is a medium brownish grey, which looks awesome with cool tone eyeshadow. I like it for a daily, flattering, softer look instead of black eyeliner. In general the dose of colours liners have some stunning super flattering colours.

Both of these eyeliners have a very limited amount of product, even more so for the Dose of Colors liner - although it has a larger amount of product (0.2 grams vs. 0.14 grams) it is shorter because the width is thicker. They are not great value and only worth using as a backup liner for your handbag for when you are out or travelling. If you are using these as your daily liner, you will go through them super quick.

The longevity of the Dose of Colors is ok but not crazy good (e.g. not as good as Inglot gel liners). It will last all day on the lid, a few hours on the waterline and inner part of the upper eyelid.

The longevity of the Clio liner is so poor I would advise against purchasing it. It smudges like crazy. How can they call it waterproof? You can’t use it even for a tiny wing, and it will transfer to your lower lid. It smudges and moves around so much, I’m not even sure it’s worth using for a smudged liner/shadow look as I think it moves around even too much for this. You’d probably be better off with a kohl pencil. It might vary by shade, as I had this experience with the choco brown shade, and the cacao brown shade seemed to perform better.

I would recommend this type of product for someone who wants to carry it in their purse to be able to do their eyeliner on the go. I don’t think it’s worth it if you are applying your makeup at home.
